Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Aqua Nails Bar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our salon, book an appointment, or receive SMS text messages from us. Our SMS program is delivered through the TurnEm salon turn-management platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name and mobile phone number, provided to us when you visit the salon or book an appointment.
  • Appointment details such as date, time, requested service, and assigned manicurist.
  • Queue and visit information needed to manage your turn at the salon.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above only to operate our salon and to send you transactional SMS messages related to your visit. We do not use your phone number for marketing or promotional SMS.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of information described in this policy exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

3. SMS Messaging Program

What we send

If you provide your mobile number to Aqua Nails Bar, you may receive transactional SMS messages including:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Notifications that your manicurist is ready or that you are next in the queue.
  • Updates about queue position or wait time.

Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on your appointments and queue activity.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your wireless carrier plan. Aqua Nails Bar does not charge a separate fee for SMS messages.

Opt-out and help

You can opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP to any message. You can request help by replying HELP. After opting out you will receive one final confirmation message and will not receive further texts. You may opt back in by contacting the salon directly.

Consent

You provide consent to receive SMS by giving your phone number to Aqua Nails Bar and agreeing to be contacted about your appointment or queue status. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of receiving services in person.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate our service:

  • Service providers — including TurnEm (queue and SMS software), Twilio (SMS delivery), and similar infrastructure providers acting on our behalf.
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety.

As stated above, mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in data are never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain client and operational data for as long as needed to provide salon services and as required by law. SMS message logs are retained only as long as needed for delivery, troubleshooting, and compliance.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), authentication for staff access, and trusted infrastructure providers. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • SMS — reply STOP to opt out at any time.
  • Access and deletion — contact us at the email or phone number below to request access to or deletion of your information.

8. Children

Our SMS program is intended for adult clients. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. A minor's information may appear in our records only when provided by a parent or guardian for the purpose of a service appointment.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our SMS program after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
